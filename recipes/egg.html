Egg

There is not a single person who hasn't eaten an egg before. This complex yet elegant dish called egg can be made in various ways. from scrambled eggs to a cake, the possibilities are endless. But today we are going to learn the hardest dish of them all, the boiled egg. Now don't click off just yet, I know making it can be a bit rough around the edges, but trust me, this tutorial will go through the entire process step by step so that you can easily make your own boiled egg.

Before starting I want to warn you beforehand that this recipe needs water! So if you haven't learn that dish already please kindly learn that first as it is a significant ingredient for making boiled egg.
