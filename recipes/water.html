Water

Water is a significant part of our life.It is so popular that people all around the world consume it all the time. It has a rich texture and a tender flavour. Water can also be used for cooking other food items. So it is important to know how to cook your own water properly. otherwise it would be very harsh if you die of starvation

Before starting anything, you need to understand that you are about to make your 1st time making a dish. So it is essential to remember that you are not inclined to make the perfect water. Making mistakes is a natural part of learning.So no matter how hard the process become, believe in yourself and try your best to make that holy glass of water.

Ingredients

  • A Glass
  • Jug
  • Pot
  • stove
  • Lighter
  • A pond

Instructions

  1. Go to a nearby pond with a pot.
  2. Fill the pot with water.
  3. Come back to your kitchen
  4. Light the stove using the lighter
  5. Put the pot on the stove
  6. Keep heating the water in medium heat
  7. Wait until the water starts boiling
  8. Turn off the stove
  9. Fill the jug with hot water
  10. Let it cool for a day
  11. Once it is all cooled up, pour the water into the glass
  12. Drink the water with your heart's content
